Edgewood is a vibrant community in Northern Pierce County, Washington. We're ideally situated near both Seattle and Tacoma. Edgewood sits atop a picturesqu platau with views of Mount Raineir, the Puyallup Valley, and Puget Sound.

Let's Touch Base

Edgewood is committed to finding partners to grow with us. Our Mayor, City Manager, and Community Development Director will all be attending ReCon 2019 in Las Vegas. Lets schedule a time to meet and discuss the opportunities to join our growing community.

Home of Opportunity

Edgewood is a city in the middle of transition from a semi-rural community to a relatively affluent suburb. Our community is a rich target for a grocery store and a handful of restaurants, which we now leave the city to find. The first movers to Edgewood will find an eager consumer base.
